About

Dr Zhaoxin Ren is a Senior Lecturer in Aerospace Engineering at Swansea University. His work connects aerospace propulsion, hydrogen and ammonia fuels, cryogenic thermofluids, combustion, safety and digital engineering for net-zero transport and energy systems.

He has led the development of HiSMART, an in-house high-fidelity simulation platform for multiphase, multi-species, reacting and non-reacting thermofluid flows across wide temperature, pressure and flow-speed conditions. The platform has been applied to gas turbines, rocket engines, scramjet engines, detonation engines, cryogenic storage and transport, thermal management systems, fuel safety and hazard assessment.

Research Areas

Cryogenic Hydrogen & Safety

Modelling of liquid and cryogenic hydrogen releases, ignition, combustion, dispersion, bunkering and infrastructure safety.

Multiphase CFD & Digital Twins

High-fidelity CFD, reduced-order modelling and physics-informed machine learning for rapid, uncertainty-aware prediction.

Propulsion & Detonation

Shock waves, rotating detonation, oblique detonation, supersonic reactive flow and advanced propulsion physics.

Thermal Management

Cryogenic heat exchangers, sustainable aviation thermal systems, flow batteries and cross-sector heat-transfer technologies.

Clean Energy Systems

Hydrogen, ammonia, battery and smart local energy systems, including simulation-led design and decision support.

Experiment-Linked Modelling

Simulation methods connected to pressure measurements, Schlieren imaging, chemiluminescence and propulsion testing.
